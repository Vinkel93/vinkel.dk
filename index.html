Om

Nicolaj is an experienced financial services consultant with a solid background in insurance and banking. His work centers on replacing or modernizing large, complex core systems—owning delivery and project management while acting as the driver who merges business needs with technology.

In practice, that means clear communication, structured execution, and a bias for pragmatic solutions that de-risk change. He’s comfortable switching between business and engineering conversations to keep momentum and align stakeholders around what matters.

Privately, Nicolaj lives outside Copenhagen with his girlfriend. When time allows, he enjoys reading, staying active, and diving into space technology.
